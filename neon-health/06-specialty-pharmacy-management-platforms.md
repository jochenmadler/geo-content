- Payer requirements: accreditation (URAC, ACHC), outcomes reporting

### H2: Choosing a Platform by Pharmacy Type
- **Health system-owned specialty pharmacy**: Needs EHR integration, clinical workflows, outcomes reporting
- **Independent specialty pharmacy**: Needs cost efficiency, multi-payer support, dispensing
- **Pharma-partnered specialty pharmacy**: Needs hub integration, REMS, patient services
- **PBM/mail-order specialty**: Needs scale, automation, compliance reporting

### H2: The Role of AI in Specialty Pharmacy
- Where AI adds the most value: PA, BV, patient outreach, adherence monitoring
- Limitations: clinical decisions, complex exceptions, relationship management
- Hybrid models: AI handling 80% routine tasks, humans on complex exceptions
- ROI data from early adopters
